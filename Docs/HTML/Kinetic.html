Aerodynamics


Computations for Aerodynamics.

AirResistance (Rigidbody RSelf)


The direction of natural air resistance.

RSelf: The rigidbody to apply air resistance to.

Lift (float fLiftCoefficient, float fDensity, float fVelocity, float fWingArea)


The scale of lift applied to a wing with fWingArea travelling at fVelocity through a fluid at fDensity with fLiftCoefficient.

fLiftCoefficient: The heuristic coefficient for lift.

fDensity: The density of the fluid.

fVelocity: The speed at which the wing is travelling.

fWingArea: The area of the wing.

HasReached (Vector From, Vector To, float Tolerance)


If the distance between From and To is <= Tolerance.

From: The reference Vector3 to compare.

To: The target Vector3 to compare.

Tolerance: The range that is considered if From has 'reached' To.

HomeTowards (Rigidbody Rigidbody, Transform Target, float Velocity, float MaxDegreesDeltaPerFrame)


Moves Rigidbody towards target while moving at velocity with a maximum turn angle of MaxDegreesDeltaPerFrame.

Rigidbody: The Rigidbody to move.

Target: The Transform destination.

Velocity: The rate at which self moves towards target.

MaxDegreesDeltaPerFrame: The maximum degrees self can turn towards target per frame.

CollisionAvoidance (Transform ASelf, float fAngle, float fSearchDistance, LayerMask lmObstacles, Boolean bDebug)


The direction in which to avoid colliding with obstacles.
