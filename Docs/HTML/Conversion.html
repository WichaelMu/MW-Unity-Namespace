ColourHex (String sHex)


Converts a hexadecimal to its corresponding colour.

sHex: The hexadecimal in the format: "#RRGGBB"; where '#' denotes a hexadecimal, 'RR' denotes the Red colour channel, 'GG' denotes the Green colour channel and 'BB' denotes the Blue colour channel.

ColourHex (String sHex, float fAlpha)


The corresponding hexadecimal and alpha colour.

sHex: The hexadecimal in the format: "#RRGGBB"; where '#' denotes a hexadecimal, 'RR' denotes the Red colour channel, 'GG' denotes the Green colour channel and 'BB' denotes the Blue colour channel.

fAlpha: The float alpha.

ColourHex (String sHex, String sAlpha)


The corresponding hexadecimal colour and hexadecimal alpha.

sHex: The hexadecimal in the format: "#RRGGBB"; where '#' denotes a hexadecimal, 'RR' denotes the Red colour channel, 'GG' denotes the Green colour channel and 'BB' denotes the Blue colour channel.

sAlpha: The hexadecimal in the format: "#AA"; where '#' denotes a hexadecimal and 'AA' denotes the Alpha channel.
