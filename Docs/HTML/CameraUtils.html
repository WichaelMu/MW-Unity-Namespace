SpringArm


A Spring Arm Component that allows a Boom to track a Target.
While commonly used as for Camera tracking, this can be used for any object that needs to track

Boom


The Transform that will track Boom.

Target


The Target for Boom to track.

TargetOffset


The offset at which Boom will track Target.

Distance


The distance in which Boom will maintain from Target whilst tracking.

Will not be used when the line between Boom and Target is interrupted by a collision.

GimbalRotation


The world-rotation of Boom, about Target + TargetOffset.

CameraRotation


The world-rotation of Boom as it looks and faces Target.

bInheritRotation


True to inherit Target's rotation.

bEnableScrollToDistance


to let the mouse wheel control

ScrollSensitivity


How much will Distance change when scrolling?

bInvertX


to invert the Controls when Orbiting about the world X-Axis.

bInvertY


to invert the Controls when Orbiting about the world Y-Axis.

bInvertZ


to invert the Zooming Distance direction.

OnlyCollideWith


Interrupt Distance when a collision is made with this layer/s.

PositionalLagStrength


The strength Boom will Lag behind tracking Target.

0 is standstill (infinite Lag). 1 is instant (no Lag).

RotationalLagStrength


The strength Boom will rotate to face Target.

0 is standstill (infinite Lag). 1 is instant (no Lag).

bUseCustomProjection


True to use a Camera Projection that cuts away close Meshes.

NearClipDistance


The distance when Meshes are clipped.

DistanceLimit


How close should the Camera be to stop using this Custom Projection?



Tracking


Utilities for a Camera following or looking at something.

CameraFollow (Camera ReferenceCamera, Transform Target)


Summary:

Have the ReferenceCamera follow Target.

Params:

ReferenceCamera: The camera to move.

Target: The target's transform component.

CameraFollow (Camera ReferenceCamera, Transform Target, Vector3 Offset)


Summary:

Have the ReferenceCamera to follow Target at an offset.

Params:

ReferenceCamera: The camera to move.

Target: The target's transform component.

Offset: The target's position at an offset.

CameraFollow (Camera ReferenceCamera, Vector3 Target)


Summary:

Have the ReferenceCamera follow Target.

Params:

ReferenceCamera: The camera to move.

Target: The target's position to follow.

CameraFollow (Camera ReferenceCamera, Vector3 Target, Vector3 Offset)


Summary:

Have the ReferenceCamera follow Target at an offset.

Params:

ReferenceCamera: The camera to move.

Target: The target's position to follow.

Offset: The target's position at an offset.

CameraFollow ()


Summary:

Have the main camera follow Target.

CameraFollow (Transform Target, Vector3 Offset)


Summary:

Have the main camera follow target's transform at an offset.

Params:

Target: The target's transform component.

Offset: The target's position at an offset.

CameraFollow ()


Summary:

Have the main camera follow Target.

CameraFollow (Vector3 Target, Vector3 Offset)


Summary:

Have the main camera follow Target at an offset.

Params:

Target: The target's position.

Offset: The target's position at an offset.

Billboard (Transform Self)


Summary:

Ensures the Self always faces the main camera.

Params:

Self: The transform to look towards the main camera.

Billboard (Transform Self, Camera ReferenceCamera)


Summary:

Ensures the Self always faces ReferenceCamera.

Params:

Self: The transform to look towards the camera.

ReferenceCamera: The camera to look at.

Billboard (Transform Self, Transform Point)


Summary:

Ensures the Self always faces Point.

Params:

Self: The transform to look towards the point.

Point: The transform of where self needs to look towards

Billboard (Transform Self, Vector3 Point)


Summary:

Ensures the Self always faces Point.

Params:

Self: The transform to look towards the point.

Point: The point in world coordinates.
