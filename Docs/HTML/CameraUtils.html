PPlane


The Plane to pan a Camera for an Orthographic world.

Pan (Camera CCamera, EButton BButtonToActivate, float fInterpolateSpeed)


Summary:

Pan CCamera using BButtonToActivate by linearlly interpolating with fInterpolateSpeed.

Params:

CCamera: The camera to pan.

BButtonToActivate: The mouse button to start activate panning.

fInterpolateSpeed: The speed to ease the camera's movement.

Tracking


Utilities for a Camera following or looking at something.

CameraFollow (Camera CCamera, Transform ATarget)


Summary:

Have the camera follow target's transform.

Params:

CCamera: The camera to move.

ATarget: The target's transform component.

CameraFollow (Camera CCamera, Transform ATarget, Vector3 vOffset)


Summary:

Have the camera to follow target's transform at an offset.

Params:

CCamera: The camera to move.

ATarget: The target's transform component.

vOffset: The target's position at an offset.

CameraFollow (Camera CCamera, Vector3 vTarget)


Summary:

Have the camera follow target's position.

Params:

CCamera: The camera to move.

vTarget: The target's position to follow.

CameraFollow (Camera CCamera, Vector3 vTarget, Vector3 vOffset)


Summary:

Have the camera follow target's position at an offset.

Params:

CCamera: The camera to move.

vTarget: The target's position to follow.

vOffset: The target's position at an offset.

CameraFollow ()


Summary:

Have the main camera follow target's transform.

CameraFollow (Transform ATarget, Vector3 vOffset)


Summary:

Have the main camera follow target's transform at an offset.

Params:

ATarget: The target's transform component.

vOffset: The target's position at an offset.

CameraFollow ()


Summary:

Have the main camera follow target's position.

CameraFollow (Vector3 vTarget, Vector3 vOffset)


Summary:

Have the main camera follow target's position at an offset.

Params:

vTarget: The target's position.

vOffset: The target's position at an offset.

Billboard (Transform ASelf)


Summary:

Ensures the transform always faces the main camera.

Params:

ASelf: The transform to look towards the main camera.

Billboard (Transform ASelf, Camera CCamera)


Summary:

Ensures the transform always faces camera.

Params:

ASelf: The transform to look towards the camera.

CCamera: The camera to look at.

Billboard (Transform ASelf, Transform APoint)


Summary:

Ensures the transform always faces point.

Params:

ASelf: The transform to look towards the point.

APoint: The transform of where self needs to look towards

Billboard (Transform ASelf, Vector3 vPoint)


Summary:

Ensures the transform always faces point.

Params:

ASelf: The transform to look towards the point.

vPoint: The point in world coordinates.
